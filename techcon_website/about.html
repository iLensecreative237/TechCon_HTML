Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for software engineers and has grown into a global event drawing thousands of attendees every year. Over the past decade, it has evolved to become a major platform for unveiling new tech trends and fostering innovation across industries.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to empower the global tech community through knowledge sharing, innovation, and meaningful connections. We strive to make technology more inclusive, accessible, and impactful for the world.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the brightest minds in technology:

  • Jane Smith – AI Pioneer and CEO of FutureTech. She spoke on ethical AI development in 2022.
  • Carlos Reyes – Open Source Advocate and Lead Developer at CodeWorld. His 2021 talk on decentralized development was widely praised.
  • Aisha Khan – Cybersecurity Expert. Delivered a keynote in 2023 on future-proofing infrastructure against threats.
